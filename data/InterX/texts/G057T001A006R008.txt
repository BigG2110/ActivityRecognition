The first person raises his left hand over the scissor hand, and then the second person also raises his hand to salute the pose.
The first person stands on the right side of the second person and briefly holds the second person's right arm with his/her left hand, then lowers his/her left hand. He/she then raises his/her right hand over the shoulder, extending all ten fingers and the middle finger. The second person raises his/her left hand over the shoulder, extending the index finger and middle finger. The two of them strike a pose together, and then lower their hands.
Two people can pose: one person with his/her right hand in front of his/her face represents scissors, while the other person with his/her left hand in front of his/her face represents scissors.
