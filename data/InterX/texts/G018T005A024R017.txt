Two people face each other, both cross their arms and bow simultaneously.
Two individuals confront each other. One person takes a seat while the other person draws near. The drawing near person inclines his/her body at a 90-degree angle to show respect to the seated person. Subsequently, the seated person rises and employs both hands to assist the approaching person in standing up.
Two people face each other. One person sits down while the other person approaches. The approaching person bends his/her body 90 degrees to bow to the seated person. Then, the seated person stands up and uses both hands to pull the approaching person up.
