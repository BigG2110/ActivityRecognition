The standing person bows to the sitting person, and then the sitting person stands up and bows to the other person.
The first person sits, while the second person stands in front of him/her. The second person puts his/her hands in front of him/her and bows to the first person. Then, the first person stands up and reciprocates the bow to the second person.
One person bends down towards the person sitting, then the person sitting stands up and bends down towards the other person.
