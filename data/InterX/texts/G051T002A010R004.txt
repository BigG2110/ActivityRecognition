One person stands while the other person sits on a chair. The standing person walks directly in front of the seated person, stretches out his/her left hand, and slaps the seated person on the right cheek. The seated person leans to the left and stretches out his/her right hand to cover his/her face.
One individual remains upright while the other individual takes a seat on a chair. The individual who is standing proceeds to move straight ahead of the individual who is seated, extends his/her left hand, and delivers a strike to the right cheek of the seated person. In response, the seated person inclines towards the left and extends his/her right hand to shield his/her face.
The first person gets up in the morning and slaps the second person in the face, resulting in him/her being slapped in the face.
